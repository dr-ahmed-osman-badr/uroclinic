Privacy Policy for Urology Department System 6 October Hospital

Last updated: December 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Urology Department System 6 October Hospital app (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

We take your privacy seriously, especially given the sensitive nature of medical data processed by this App. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that is necessary for the functionality of the Hospital Management System. This includes:

A. Patient Data

The App allows authorized medical personnel to input and store:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, Gender, Date of Birth, Insurance Number, Telephone Numbers.
  • Medical Information: Medical History, Surgical History, Current Diagnosis, viral markers (e.g., HCV, HBV), and other health-related notes.
  • Admission & Operation Details: Dates of admission/discharge, planned operations, surgeon notes, and booking slots.

B. App Permissions

To provide its features, the App may request access to:

  • Storage / Files: To read and write medical reports, export data (e.g., Excel/PDF), or upload patient-related documents.
  • Network / Internet: To communicate with our secure hospital server (drahmedosmanbadr.pythonanywhere.com) to synchronize data.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used strictly for:

  • Hospital Management: Tracking patient admissions, scheduling operations, and maintaining medical records.
  • Communication: Facilitating coordination between doctors and hospital staff regarding patient care.
  • Record Keeping: Maintaining a digital archive of patient history and operations.

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent patient personal identification information to others.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Server: Data is stored on a secure server. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect against unauthorized access.
  • Local Storage: Some ephemeral data may be stored locally on your device to improve performance or strictly for offline functionality if supported.
  • Access Control: Access to this App is restricted to authorized medical staff of 6 October Hospital.

5. Third-Party Services

This App may rely on third-party services for specific functionality:

  • Hosting Provider: The backend API is hosted on PythonAnywhere.
  • Google Play Services: Used for app distribution and core device features.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is a professional tool intended for use by hospital staff. It is not designed for use by children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
